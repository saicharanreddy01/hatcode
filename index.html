Why it matters

Bad data doesn't announce itself

It hides in nulls you didn't catch, duplicates that slipped through, and metrics that drifted silently for weeks.

Silent pipeline failures

Bad rows don't error loudly — they propagate downstream, corrupt reports, and surface weeks later when the damage is done.

Fix it before it's downstream.

Duplicates you don't know exist

Near-duplicate customer records, orders entered twice, typos that look like new entries — they skew every metric you trust.

Catch them before they compound.

Anomalies that cost money

An average order amount that jumped 98×. A null rate that tripled overnight. Without a baseline, you'll never know until it's expensive.

Know before it escalates.
How it works

Connect. Scan. Know.

Three steps. Under two minutes from signup to your first quality report.

01

Connect your database

Point HatCode at PostgreSQL, MySQL, or SQLite. No agents, no installs — just a connection string and you're in.

02

Run a scan

HatCode profiles every column, applies your quality rules, hunts for duplicates, and establishes anomaly baselines — automatically.

03

Get a full quality report

A health score, every violation ranked by severity, near-duplicate pairs, and a z-score for anything that shifted from baseline.
